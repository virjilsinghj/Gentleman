1. STEP 1:

  2. Choose social media marketing goals that align to business objectives

  3. Set S.M.A.R.T. goals:

  4. The first step to creating a winning strategy is to establish your objectives and goals. Without goals, you have no way to measure success and return on investment (ROI).
  5. Each of your goals should be:
    • Specific
    • Measurable
    • Attainable
    • Relevant
    • Time-bound

  6. Track meaningful metrics:

  7. Vanity metrics like number of followers and likes are easy to track, but it’s hard to prove their real value. Instead, focus on things like engagement, click-through, and conversion rates.
  8. You may want to track different goals for different networks, or even different uses for each network.
  9. For example, if you use LinkedIn to drive traffic to your website, you would measure click-throughs. If Instagram is for brand awareness, you might track the number of Instagram Story views. And if you advertise on Facebook, cost-per-click (CPC) is a common success metric.
  10. Social media goals should align with your overall marketing objectives. This makes it easier to show the value of your work and secure buy-in from your boss.

  12. Start developing your social media marketing plan by writing down at least three goals for social media.
